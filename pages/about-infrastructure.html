Campus Infrastructure Overview

Academic Growth

Faculty of Science, Management Studies, and Computer Science were introduced with required cutting-edge laboratories to keep pace with growing academic demands.

Major Additions

Immense additions include an 800-seater auditorium, Evaluation & Secrecy cells, Administrative block, Central Library, underground parking, girls' hostel, and sports grounds.

Student Facilities

Every academic block features dedicated office and common rooms, with separate well-maintained common rooms and washrooms for female students, male students, and staff.

Inclusivity & Access

Fully accessible campus with elevators and ramps. Special facilities include audio books, screen-readers, braille displays, and specialized support for dyslexic and hearing-impaired students.
