About the Cell

Shaping Careers,
Building Futures

The Training & Placement Cell of NPGC aims to ensure students secure suitable employment and launch successful careers by facilitating job placements, bridging the gap between academia and industry through enhanced employability skills, career guidance, and dedicated support.

The cell ensures a smooth recruitment process for both students and visiting companies — organizing drives, job fairs, internships, and training programs for final year and other eligible students.
