Getting Help

How to Access

1

Walk In or Register

Visit the Placement Cell office or register online for upcoming guidance sessions and counselling camps.

2

Share Your Goals

Discuss your academic background, interests and aspirations with our career team — no judgement, just guidance.

3

Get Your Roadmap

Receive a personalised action plan with recommended skills, exams, certifications and timeline to achieve your goal.

4

Follow Up & Grow

Return for follow-up sessions as you progress. The placement cell stays with you through your entire final year journey.
