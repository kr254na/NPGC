Getting Started

How to Enrol

1

Check Notice Board

Workshop announcements are posted on the NPGC placement board and sent via WhatsApp / email.

2

Register & Pay

Fill the registration form with your Roll No. and pay the nominal workshop fee (if applicable) at the placement cell.

3

Attend & Participate

Attend all sessions, complete hands-on assignments and actively engage with the faculty and activities.

4

Collect Certificate

Receive your participation or completion certificate from Imarticus / NPGC after the workshop concludes.
